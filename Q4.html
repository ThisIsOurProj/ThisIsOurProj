Our SEO Plan

Renaming video file

First, we will rename our video file using a target keyword so youtube can read our video's file name and all the code that comes with it when it's uploaded

Since we are planning to create a gaming video using Among Us, we should include ‘Among Us’ ‘gaming’ and the stuff or content that we will be doing in that game, since our plan is to try different modes in among us and at least 3 different maps in it then we should create like a phrase for that.

So for our video file “Among Us gameplay trying different modes and maps”

Inserting the keywords naturally in our title

LoAfter that, we will try to put the keywords in the title of our video but we will try to not make it awkward and make it clear and concise. We also researched and look into popular gaming videos, specifically among us and we compare the title of the videos one another, we noticed that they all create a title wherein they will say what they’re going to do, the title is usually long and like it will create some sort of curiosity, they also use “!” and “...” at the end.

With that, we decided to name our second video “We tried playing different modes and maps in Among Us but….. In -969690 IQ!”

Optimizing our video description

We will also optimize our video description for a better understanding of our audience towards our video. But we won’t make it too long since the priority of our audiences is to watch our video.

So the plan for our video introduction is to like show why we created this video and our experience in creating this video

Tagging our video with popular keywords

Next is tagging our video with popular keywords that relate to our topic.

Some of the keywords that we will use: #amongus #gaming #gameplay #fun

Categorizing our video

We will also categorize our video based on our content, this will greatly help us with algorithms and being clear about our video.

So our category is 'gaming video category'

Thumbnail

After that we will also create a capturing thumbnail that also captures what our video is all about.

In the thumbnail, there would be the Among Us characters and also the maps, etc.

Subtitles

We will also add subtitles to our video, we will most likely put English subs since we will be speaking in Taglish and so that other audiences that don’t speak or understand Tagalog can still watch our video

End screen

Lastly, we would also add some end screens, we would put there our previous video and the channel of our partners to gain more engagement.
