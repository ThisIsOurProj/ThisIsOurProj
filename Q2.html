Brand Name

Hello we are Nui Moai!

Nui means great/big/abundant and Moai is a head statue, you might know Moai as the stoned face statue.

Why not? Moai is such a cool dude, he's a head statue.

It's not the first 10 names that we have thought of , we discovered our brand name through a kind of a joke. We were asking for suggestion for the brand name then one of our member just responded with the stoned face emoji (at first we didn't know what it's really called) so we just joked that, that emoji will be our brand name then we really make our brand name that emoji , later on we researched 'what is the name of the stoned emoji' then we discovered that it's called 'Moai' then one of our member added Nui to it.

OUR LOGO

We planned to put the Moai head there since it’s our brand name

We only used one big triangle for our logo, integrated the triangle in our logo by thinking of what fits the Moai, we thought that Moai is “ancienty” we thought of another thing that is also “ancienty” that has like a triangle figure and we thought of the illuminati and we tried to combine the both of them and it worked, so our logo is like “the illuminati of Moai”

Both dimension of the sides of triangle (EF & EG) measures 8 cm , for the bottom part of the triangle(FG) it also measures 8cm For the angle of m∠EFG = 60 degrees , for the angle of m∠EGF = 60 degrees, and for the angle of m∠FEG = 60 degrees .

The exterior angle is 120 degrees.

Our triangle also satisfied the Triangle Properties.
